Advanced Spatio-Temporal Analysis & Visualization of Rainfall Dynamics

This project focuses on analyzing and visualizing rainfall patterns in Washington State, an area known for its significant precipitation. Utilizing GIS, the initiative will provide time-lapse visualizations to reveal trends and anomalies in rainfall dynamics. This analytical tool, primarily based on NOAA's weather station data, aims to offer critical insights for researchers, policymakers, and environmentalists. Future phases may explore the integration of predictive analytics to enhance the tool's capabilities.
